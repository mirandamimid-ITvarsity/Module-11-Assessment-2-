La colombe

Drinks There’s a lengthy wine list that comprises a mix of local and international names, with a pleasant focus on wines from the surrounding Franschhoek area and beyond. You can opt to have a paired wine tasting with your meal, or choose by the bottle or carafe. Service Impeccable, attentive, warm and knowledgeable – the service here is the standard to which all front-of-house teams should aspire. Ambience Set in a glass-windowed dining room, you’re surrounded by natural light and peeks of the Franschhoek mountains. Best for… A celebratory blowout meal.

Boisdale of Mayfair

Located in converted listed stables on North Row, off Park Lane, Boisdale of Mayfair restaurant offers a wide selection of dishes inspired by traditional British cuisine. The ground level Stable Bar includes a draft ale, an extensive wine list and a choice of fine and rare malts of whisky. Guests are accompanied by nightly jazz and blues piano performances.
